About

From idea to intelligent system

I'm a second-year Industrial Computer Engineering student at ENET'Com (École Nationale d'Électronique et des Télécommunications de Sfax), working at the intersection of software engineering, embedded systems, artificial intelligence and automation.

Engineering, for me, is more than a profession — it's the process of creating solutions that never existed before. Every project is a chance to learn, innovate and push my technical limits, whether that means wiring a sensor to a microcontroller or orchestrating a multi-agent RAG pipeline.
